Topics on which articles are needed

Research articles on these topics are needed. These can then be publiched in a book format. These are great freedom fighters, who were part of Indian Independence, Hyderabad Viomochan. Unfortunately these people have not been highlighted. They are unknown, unsung. You can choose a topic and write a detailed article. Please contact us so that we can assisst you in which ever way we can.

Ekatmata Stotram through Stamps 2025

Ekatmata Stotram (Bharata Bhakti stotram) has many eminient personalities, historical, holy places, sacred rivers.. Govt of India has released postal stamps on these from time to time. We compiled and printed this book in 2025. Sai Vara Prasad, Bhagyanagar Unit president was instrumental in gathering stamps & compiling and bringing this book.
