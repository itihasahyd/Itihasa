Seminar in Andhra Mahila Sabha

We conducted a seminar on Unsung Heroes of Hyderabad Vimochan along with Andhra Mahila Sabha Degree College, Vidyanagar, Hyderabad on 16 July 2022. 25 papers were submitted
Maharashtra National Law University Vice Chancellor Prof.K.V.S.Sharma was the chief Guest and inaugarated the seminar
Potti Sree Ramulu Telugu University Vice Chancellor Prof.T.Kishan Rao was the Valedictorian speaker bringing the seminar to a close
